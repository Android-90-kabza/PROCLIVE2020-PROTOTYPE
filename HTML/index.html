Background

Beginning in 2012, Tshepo always kept busy DIY'ing projects for intimate family and friends. Given the multiple RFQ's from potential clients throughout 2020 - a year of uncertainty and lack of job security - we identified an opportunity to structure a corporate entity out of our DIY passion.

Proclive2020 is a passionate provider of construction services that include, maintenance, renovations, and installations. Our specialty is in carpentry works, laminate and other floorings and ceiling installations. Our work and methodology is an expression of passionate service with the a client centric mindset.
